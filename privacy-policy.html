Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Read Aloud Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our users while providing a helpful text-to-speech service.

Read Aloud is designed with privacy in mind, collecting only the minimum information necessary to provide its core functionality.

Required Permissions

Read Aloud requires the following permissions to function properly:

storage

Why we need it: This permission allows the extension to save your preferences (such as reading speed, pitch, and voice selection) locally on your device. This ensures your settings persist between browsing sessions.

activeTab

Why we need it: This permission gives the extension access to the currently active tab to extract text content for reading aloud and to apply word highlighting. The extension only accesses the tab when you explicitly activate it.

contextMenus

Why we need it: This permission allows the extension to add the "Read from this text" option to your right-click context menu. This enables you to start reading from any selected text on a webpage.

Information We Collect

Read Aloud collects and processes the following information:

  • User Preferences: Your settings for reading speed, pitch, and preferred voice are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.
  • Temporary Text Content: When you use the extension, it temporarily processes the text content of the webpage you're viewing to provide the text-to-speech functionality.

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials
  • Any data from websites you visit
  • Any analytics or usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide the core text-to-speech functionality
  • Apply word highlighting as text is read
  • Remember your preferred settings
  • Enable the right-click context menu functionality

We do not use your information for any other purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share any information with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

All user preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

The text content that is read aloud is processed entirely within your browser and is not stored permanently or transmitted elsewhere.

Third-Party Services

Read Aloud does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs beyond what is provided by the Chrome browser itself. The text-to-speech functionality uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API.

User Rights

As a user of Read Aloud, you have the right to:

  • Change or delete your preferences at any time through the extension's settings
  • Uninstall the extension, which will remove all locally stored preferences

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
