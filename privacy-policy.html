Privacy Policy

Read Aloud Chrome Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Read Aloud Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our extension.

Information Collection

The Read Aloud extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data to external servers. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

Local Storage: The extension uses Chrome's storage API to save your preferences (such as voice selection, speech rate, and pitch) locally on your device. This information never leaves your browser and is only used to remember your settings between sessions.

Permissions

The Read Aloud extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your voice and speech settings locally
  • activeTab: To access and read the content of your current tab
  • contextMenus: To add the "Read from this text" option to your right-click menu

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension and not for collecting or transmitting data.

Text Processing

When you use the Read Aloud extension, it processes the text on the web page you're viewing to convert it to speech. This processing happens entirely within your browser using the Web Speech API. The text content is never sent to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

The Read Aloud extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The speech synthesis is performed using your browser's built-in Web Speech API, which may use your operating system's text-to-speech capabilities.

Children's Privacy

The Read Aloud extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. As the extension does not collect personal information from any users, it is suitable for use by people of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
